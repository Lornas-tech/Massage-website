About

My name is Lorna. I'm a certified and licensed massage therapist in the states of CT, Hawaii, Maine and Massachusetts. I specialize in Swedish, Deep tissue, Thai Yoga Massage, Cupping, Acupressure, Medical Massage and Reflexology. I have also received a Reiki certification in Varkala, India in 2019. I have six years of experience working in a variety of settings, including chiropractic offices, five-star spas, day spas, but currently work as travel massage therapist.

Healing is a process that is unique for each individual. Understanding the metaphysical as well as physical aspects of healing is very important to bring about the necessary changes in order to return to homeostasis.

Massage has many benefits to the body and mind. It has been used for thousands of years to reduce stress, improve circulation, increase joint flexibility and mobility, stimulate the lymph system, lessen pain, strengthen the immune system...etc.

"The best way to health is to have an aromatic bath and a scented massage every day." -Hippocrates
